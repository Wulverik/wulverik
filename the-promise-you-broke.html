The Promise You Broke

Chapter I of the Glitched Wolf’s journey — a song of fractured trust, truth, and becoming.

This chapter was born from a broken bond — not to call anyone out, but to honor what it feels like when loyalty is misread as “too much,” and truth is twisted into a lie. It is the moment the Glitched Wolf realized that even the deepest hurt can become a spark.

Meaning & Echoes

“You said you’d be my voice. When the world turned away.”
Someone once promised to stand beside the Wolf — to defend him, speak for him, and understand him when others did not. It felt like loyalty. It felt like safety.

“I thought our friendship was something that would stay.”
This is the hope that a rare connection will last — the belief that this bond is different.

“I trusted you with truth… But you called my care obsession.”
Vulnerability meets misinterpretation. Care is twisted into “clinginess,” loyalty into something unstable. The hurt begins here.

Pre-Chorus — “what you called clingy was me fighting the pain…”
The Wolf wasn’t clinging out of weakness, but out of empathy — trying to support someone they cared about through shared pain.

“You said I was lying, but my heart stayed true.”
False accusations cut deeper than distance. This line holds the ache of being called a liar when you know you’ve been honest.

“You twisted my words till I barely knew.”
This reflects gaslighting energy — when your reality gets twisted so much that you start to doubt your own perception.

“You called it clingy, I called it loyal.”
The core contrast of the song: two different interpretations of the same behavior. One sees burden. The other sees devotion.

“Now the ones who whispered the lies, are in the dark.”
Rumors and whispers feel powerful in the moment, but they don’t last. Truth doesn’t always shout, but it survives.

“You sacrificed friendship. Someone who was true.”
The pain of realizing that the other person chose one-sided stories over the relationship you were actually living.

“I gave you my fears, but you let them drown.”
Opening up about fears and trauma — and watching them sink because the other person couldn’t or wouldn’t hold them.

“You smiled through the cracks while tearing me down.”
A gentle mask hiding sharp edges. The disconnect between how someone presents and how their choices actually feel.

“But you cut the rope and left me to rise.”
The turning point. Being abandoned hurts — but it also forces growth. The Wolf learns to stand without their support.

Bridge — “You made me doubt my world, my voice, my spark.”
When someone you trust questions your reality, it can shake your sense of self. This line names that collapse.

“But I’m still standing. Rebuilding from dark.”
This is the rise — quiet, stubborn resilience. Not pretending it didn’t hurt, but choosing to rebuild anyway.

“Because of you, your mark is now my spark.”
The alchemy of pain. The wound becomes fuel. The mark they left becomes the fire that drives the Wolf forward.

Outro — “I will keep my promise… A candle lit in the dark.”
The Wolf remains loyal to who he is, even if the other person is gone. The door is open, but he won’t knock. The candle symbolizes a promise kept without expectation — a quiet act of love, and a refusal to let hate win.

“The Promise You Broke” is not about revenge — it’s about recognition. If you’ve ever been painted as “too much,” “too clingy,” or “the villain” for caring deeply, this chapter is for you. Your loyalty was never the problem. The wrong story was.
