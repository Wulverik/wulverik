YOU ARE NOT ALONE

If you are in darkness right now, please don't face it alone.
This realm is a story-world. The people and links below are real-world lifelines.
If you are in immediate danger or feel you may act on your thoughts:
Please contact your local emergency number right now (such as 911, 112, 999, or your country's equivalent), or use one of the crisis lines below. Your safety matters more than anything else.
Important

Wulverik is a realm of music, story, and symbolism, created by an artist who has walked through their own darkness. It is not therapy, medical advice, or professional crisis support.

I am not a doctor, therapist, or emergency service. I cannot safely or responsibly act as a crisis line. That's why this page exists: so you always have real-world options if you or someone you care about is struggling.

Global & International Resources

These organizations can help you find support in many different countries and regions:

Worldwide Directories

Befrienders Worldwide:
Emotional support organizations in many countries. Website: befrienders.org

International Suicide Hotlines (OpenCounseling):
A directory of suicide prevention hotlines by country. Website: opencounseling.com/suicide-hotlines

If you don't see your country listed below, these links are a good place to start.

United States

988 Suicide & Crisis Lifeline:
Call or text 988, or chat via 988lifeline.org (24/7).

Crisis Text Line:
Text HOME to 741741 to reach a trained crisis counselor (24/7).

Canada

Talk Suicide Canada:
Call 1-833-456-4566 or text 45645 (hours may vary by region). More info at talksuicide.ca .

United Kingdom & Ireland

Samaritans:
Call 116 123 (free, 24/7) in the UK & Ireland, or visit samaritans.org .

Australia

Lifeline Australia:
Call 13 11 14 or visit lifeline.org.au .

Beyond Blue:
Call 1300 22 4636 or visit beyondblue.org.au .

New Zealand

Lifeline Aotearoa:
Call 0800 543 354 or text HELP to 4357.

India

AASRA:
Call +91 9820466726 or visit aasra.info .

South Africa

Suicide Crisis Line:
Call 0800 567 567 or SMS 31393.

Philippines

Hopeline (Philippines):
Phone numbers and info: opencounseling.com/suicide-hotlines/ph .

Singapore

Samaritans of Singapore (SOS):
Call 1767 or visit sos.org.sg .

Japan

TELL Lifeline:
Call 03-5774-0992 or visit telljp.com .

South Korea

Lifeline Korea / local hotlines:
See a current list of numbers at opencounseling.com/suicide-hotlines/kr .

If your country isn't listed, please check the global directories above, or search "suicide hotline" + your country name. Many countries also list mental health crisis lines on their government health websites.

You are not weak for needing help. You are human. Wulverik is here to give you stories, symbols, and songs — but when the shadows feel too heavy, please reach for living voices too.
