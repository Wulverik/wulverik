Lore & Echoes

The Codex of Wulverik — where memory, myth, and truth are written.

Lore & Echoes is where the Glitched Wolf’s story is kept. This realm holds the memories behind the music, the weight of old scars, the sparks of healing, and the way an autistic mind experiences a world that never stops humming.

Here, you’ll share the echoes behind your songs — the friendships that changed you, the betrayals that shook you, the quiet victories, and the moments where you refused to let the world turn your heart to stone.

What This Realm Can Hold

As Wulverik grows, this realm can become a living codex:

  • Stories from your life that shaped key songs
  • Reflections on autism, sensitivity, and being “too much” for the world
  • Philosophy, quotes, and ideas that anchor you
  • Short lore pieces that blend your real history with Wulverik’s myth

Between Reality and Myth

Some entries here might be raw and honest. Others might sound like legend. Both are true. Wulverik is the bridge between the life you’ve lived and the world you’re building to hold it — a place where even the hardest chapters can transform into stories that help others feel less alone.

This page is just the first page of the Codex. Later, you can add sections for different eras, people, and songs — turning Lore & Echoes into a map of everything the Glitched Wolf has survived.
