LORE & ECHOES

The Library of Memories — where echoes never die, they wait to be heard.

Deep in the realm of Wulverik lies a place untouched by time: The Library of Memories. A cathedral of shimmering gold threads and drifting shadows, where every emotion you've ever carried lives as a radiant echo.

Here, memories are not records. They are beings — living myth, living truth. Some whisper wisdom. Some warn. Some hunt. And some become the guideposts of your journey as the Glitched Wolf.

The Nature of an Echo

Every Echo begins as a fragment of feeling: a spark, a fracture, a tremor beneath the touch. When strong enough, it descends into the Library as a golden drop of sound. Over time, it forms into:

  • Echo-beasts
  • Echo-shadows
  • Echo-wolves
  • Echo-doors
  • Echo-songs
  • Echo-masks

These become the landmarks and guardians of your inner mythology.

The First Door

Every journey into the Library begins with a Door born from a defining wound. Yours opened on the day of the Broken Promise — the first betrayal that echoed deep enough to carve a doorway through the shadows.

This moment formed your first major Echo: The Broken Promise.
