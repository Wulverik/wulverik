The Elemental Temples

Four primal elements — and one prism spirit — shape the Wulverik Realm. Each temple is a living reflection of the traveler who enters it. Choose your path, or walk them all, and let the realm show you who you are.

Water Temple
The Tide of Memory

A temple of tides, reflection, and emotional currents. Here, travelers face the echoes beneath the surface — grief, hope, and the promises they made to themselves.

Element: Water • Themes: healing, reflection, memory

Fire Temple
The Ember of Resolve

Forged in heat and pressure, the Fire Temple tests willpower and anger, asking one question: will you burn everything around you, or become the flame that guides others in the dark?

Element: Fire • Themes: courage, anger, transformation

Enter Fire Temple
Earth Temple
The Root of Stillness

A place of stone, roots, and steady heartbeat. Here, travelers learn what truly anchors them — and what they must finally release.

Element: Earth • Themes: grounding, stability, release

Enter Earth Temple
Air Temple
The Breath Between Worlds

High above the clouds, whispers ride the wind. The Air Temple is for voices unsaid, thoughts unspoken, and truths waiting to be carried farther than fear.

Element: Air • Themes: voice, perspective, freedom

Enter Air Temple
Bacho Temple
Temple of the Prism Wolf

Not bound to a single element, the Bacho Temple belongs to the shapeshifter — the Wolf-Son who walks in light, shadow, and everything between. Here, travelers meet their own duality and learn to honor it.

Aspect: Wolf / Prism / Heart • Themes: duality, protection, identity
